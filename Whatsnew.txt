Intelligent Resume Builder — Now Available

We're excited to bring you Intelligent Resume Builder: an AI-powered resume and cover letter app designed from the ground up to work well for everyone, including blind and low-vision users.

What you can do:
- Generate a complete, professional resume with AI, in your choice of 10 formats: PDF, DOCX, RTF, TXT, HTML, ODT, DOC, Markdown, XML, or JSON Resume
- Watch your resume get written in real time with live AI streaming
- Use the AI Live Editor to update any detail with a plain text instruction, no forms required
- Get an AI ATS Score and Feedback report to see how applicant-tracking software will read your resume
- Generate a matching AI Cover Letter, with tone, hiring manager name, and key achievements built in
- Have any resume or letter read aloud using built-in text-to-speech
- Pick your own AI provider: Gemini, OpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter
- Write your resume in English or any custom language you choose
- Back up and restore all your resumes in one tap

Intelligent Resume Builder is built by Open Vision Engineering, with accessibility treated as a first-class feature, not an afterthought.
